FABRICS




Double Bed Bedsheets
  • It's essential to have a couple of sets of bedsheets for your dorm or apartment. Opt for comfortable and easy-to-maintain materials like cotton or microfiber. Double bed size is suitable for most standard dorm beds.

  • With pillow covers

Mattress and Pillows
  • Depending on your college's accommodation, you may need to arrange for a mattress and pillows.

  • Check if your college provides them, or consider purchasing them for added comfort and a good night's sleep.

  • Look for options that suit your preferences in terms of firmness and support.

Raincoats and Umbrellas
  • Consider having a raincoat or umbrella to protect yourself from unexpected rain showers.

  • This is especially important because of frequent rainfall. Being prepared for weather changes will keep you comfortable and prevent disruptions to your daily activities.
